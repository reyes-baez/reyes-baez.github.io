I am a Research and Teaching FSE fellow at the RuG.

Research Interests

I am mainly interested in the theory, engineering, technology and education of systems and control, with an emphasis on applications in modern technology.

Specifically:

  • Lyapunov, contractivity and passivity techniques in control of nonlinear systems

  • Lagranian and port-Hamiltonian methods for modeling and control of physcal systems and networks

  • Learning for dynamics and control: control-informed NNs, iterative learning

  • Robotics and mechatronics: motion control of rigid and soft robots, high-precision control

  • Applications: opto-mechatronics, biomedical engineering, wind energy systems, smart industry
